Media relations

We welcome journalists to cover our event, and provide a unique atmosphere to it


The entire event will be covered by the videography department, hence, external photographers and media recording sessions will not be allowed during the talks. However, we will consider any such request.
If you are interested in covering our event, please contact Anamika Sethi.

It is also upto the speaker's discretion if he/she would like to be featured or not in any article or newspiece.
Mobile phones will need to be switched off during the proceedings of the event.
Usage of any images of the speakers in a derogatory manner will not be tolerated.
